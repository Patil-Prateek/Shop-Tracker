# Retail Management & POS System

> A cross-platform retail management and POS application that enables real-time employee sales tracking, inventory management, billing, and cloud synchronization. Designed with an offline-first architecture, it helps retailers manage sales, stock, employees, and business operations from anywhere.

---

# 📌 Overview

Managing a retail business often involves multiple employees handling sales independently while inventory is tracked manually. This project aims to digitize that workflow by providing a centralized platform where every sale is synchronized in real time.

Employees can record sales from their own devices, while owners and managers can monitor inventory, employee performance, and overall business activity through a unified dashboard.
